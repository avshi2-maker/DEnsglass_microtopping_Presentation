## Static Tabs (12–16) — Important Rule

Tabs 12–16 are skipped by the forEach loop:
```js
const STATIC_KEYS = ['podcast','workplan','team','sitephotos','process'];
if(STATIC_KEYS.includes(sl.slide_key)) return;
```
They are appended separately at the end of renderAll().
Their rows MUST exist in presentation_slides but will NOT be rendered by forEach.
